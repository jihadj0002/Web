Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package in real-time.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and with all tags attached. Some exclusions apply for special items. Please visit our Returns page for complete details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. During checkout, you'll see the available shipping options and costs for your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ShopEasy gift cards. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How do I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team by phone at (123) 456-7890, by email at support@shopeasy.com, or through our live chat feature available on our website during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm EST).

Do you have physical stores?

Currently, we operate as an online-only retailer. This allows us to keep our prices competitive while offering a wider selection of products than traditional brick-and-mortar stores. However, we're exploring physical locations in select cities.

Didn't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly and we'll be happy to help.
